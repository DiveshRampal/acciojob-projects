The Beagle Dog

The beagle is breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. processing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinct, the beagle is primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quaratine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
